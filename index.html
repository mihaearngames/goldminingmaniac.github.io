Account Deletion Request Instructions

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Gold Mining Maniac, please follow the steps below: Verify Your Identity: Please enter your in-game username and the registered email address associated with your account. Submit a Deletion Request: Fill out the deletion request form on our website at: https://www.yourdomain.com/account-deletion (Replace this URL with the actual link where you host the deletion request form.) On this page, you will be asked to confirm that you want to delete your account and all related data, which includes your game statistics, settings, and any transaction history. Processing and Retention: Once your request is submitted, our support team will process your account deletion. Please note that: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our active systems. For security and backup purposes, certain data may be retained in our backup systems for up to 30 days before being permanently removed. Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once your account has been successfully deleted. Further Inquiries: If you have any questions or concerns regarding the deletion of your account or data, please contact us at: info.miha.games@gmail.com
